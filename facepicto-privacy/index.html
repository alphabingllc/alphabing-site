FacePicto Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2025

Introduction

At FacePicto, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how the FacePicto mobile application ("App") handles your data. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

No Data Collection

FacePicto does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user photos to any external server. All image processing and face detection are performed locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework. For more details, see Apple's Face Detection API overview.

Face Detection and Image Use

When you select a group photo in the app, FacePicto uses Apple's on-device Vision API to detect face bounding boxes, which are rectangular coordinates showing where faces appear in the photo. The app does not collect biometric data, facial landmarks, or personally identifiable facial data.

This face data is used only to organize and display faces in list, grid, or video formats. You may choose to label each face with a name or title to help organize your content. All of this data remains private and local to your device. At no point is your photo or face data transmitted, uploaded, or stored on any external server. All processing takes place entirely on your device.

Data Storage and Retention

Images and face data are stored temporarily within the app's private user profile memory, which is not accessible by any other apps. This storage allows you to work across multiple sessions. Data is automatically cleared when a new image is selected. You can also remove all stored image and face-related data manually using the "Delete" button provided on the app's home screen.

Photo Access and Permissions

FacePicto requires access to your photo library only so you can choose a photo for editing. The app does not upload or share any images outside of your device. You can change photo access permissions at any time through your device settings.

No Tracking or External Sharing

FacePicto does not include analytics, tracking SDKs, third-party services, or server-side processing. All data and actions remain entirely private and local to your device.

Screen Recording and Sharing

You may optionally record or share screen content using your device's built-in features. For example, you can showcase labeled faces or lists created in the app. Please ensure you comply with the guidelines of any platform you share on.

Access to This Policy

This Privacy Policy is accessible at any time from the app's home screen and is also available online.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App constitutes your acceptance of any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@alphabing.com.

Privacy Policy for FacePicto

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16 2026

Introduction

At FacePicto, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the FacePicto mobile application (“App”) on iOS or Android. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Your Data Stays on Your Device

All photos, face detection, and content creation in FacePicto happen entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your photos, face data, or personal information to any server. Nothing leaves your device.

Face detection is performed using on-device frameworks (Apple Vision on iOS, ML Kit on Android). No facial data is shared with us or any third party.

In-App Purchases

FacePicto offers an optional HD Export upgrade as a one-time in-app purchase. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store any payment information. Purchase history is managed by Apple or Google under their respective privacy policies.

Advertising (Android Only)

The Android version of FacePicto uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data to serve relevant advertising. For details, see Google’s Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device privacy settings.

The iOS version of FacePicto does not display ads.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Store — app distribution and in-app purchases (iOS)
  • Google Play Store — app distribution and in-app purchases (Android)
  • Google AdMob — advertising (Android only)

Data We Do Not Collect

To be clear, FacePicto does NOT collect:

  • Photos or images
  • Face detection data or facial features
  • Names or personal details entered in the app
  • Location data
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Device identifiers (on iOS)

Children

FacePicto is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at contact@alphabing.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

What face data does the app collect?

The app uses Apple’s Vision framework (iOS) and ML Kit (Android), which process images entirely on-device, to detect face bounding boxes. No biometric data, facial landmarks, or personally identifiable information is collected or stored.

How is the face data used?

Face data is used only within the app to visually mark detected faces and help you organize them in list, grid, or video formats. You may optionally assign a name or title to each face. At no point is your photo or face data transmitted, uploaded, or stored on any external server.

Is face data shared with third parties?

No. Face data is stored locally on your device in the app’s private storage, not accessible by other apps.

How long is face data retained?

Face data is kept until you select a new photo or tap the delete button, which clears all stored image and face data.

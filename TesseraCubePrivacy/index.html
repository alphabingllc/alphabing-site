TesseraCube Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15 2024

Introduction

At TesseraCube, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the TesseraCube mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

No Data Collection

TesseraCube does not collect any personal data or user-generated content. Our App is designed to provide an engaging and challenging puzzle experience without requiring any personal information from our users.

Usage by Children and Adults

TesseraCube is suitable for use by both children and adults. We understand that children may use the App with the consent of their parents or guardians. If a child initiates a payment within the App, parents or guardians should contact Apple support for assistance with payment-related issues, such as cancellations or refunds.

All payment and transaction data are managed by Apple, and Apple's Privacy Policy governs their handling of personal information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy here: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

Third-Party Services

TesseraCube utilizes Apple services for processing in-app purchases and subscriptions. We do not have access to any personal information related to these transactions. For information on how Apple handles your data, please review Apple's Privacy Policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

Screen Recording and Sharing

Users may record their screen while using TesseraCube and share these recordings on platforms such as YouTube or educational websites. This feature is intended to allow users to showcase their gameplay and learning progress. Please ensure that any shared content adheres to the terms and conditions of the platform where it is uploaded.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date at the top of the Privacy Policy will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the App following any updates signifies acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@alphabing.com.

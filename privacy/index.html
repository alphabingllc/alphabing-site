Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 09 2023

Introduction

At Alphabing, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Alphabing mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

No Data Collection

Alphabing does not collect any personal data or user-generated content. Our App is designed to provide an enjoyable and educational experience for learning alphabets of foreign languages without requiring any personal information from our users.

Usage by Children and Adults

Alphabing is designed to be used by both children and adults for learning alphabets of foreign languages. While our App is not specifically targeted towards children under the age of 13, we recognize that children may use the App with their parents' or guardians' permission.

If a child initiates a payment within the App, parents or guardians should contact Apple support for any payment-related issues, including cancellations or refunds. All payment and transaction data are handled by Apple, and their use of your personal information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, which can be found at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses Apple services for processing in-app purchases and subscriptions. We do not have access to your personal information related to these transactions. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ for details on how they handle your personal data.

Screen Recording and Sharing

Users are permitted to record their screens while using the App and share these recordings on YouTube or educational websites to showcase their learning progress or for educational purposes. Please ensure that any shared content complies with the terms and conditions of the platform on which it is uploaded.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes to the Privacy Policy will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy will be revised. Your continued use of the App after any changes to the Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@alphabing.com.
